Resources

This page has links to websites that can help on the journey on the path of learning the guitar.

Justin Guitar

Justin guitar is a great website that teaches and guides you to the absolute basics of learning the gutiar, it offers beginners, intermediate and advance techniques all for free.

songsterr

songsterr is a website where you can learn guitar, bass, and drum tabs and cords.

Ultimate Guitar

Ultimate Guitar is a website where you can learn and read guitar tabs.

FlashCords

FlashCords is from the website GoCords, that test your knowlege on what cords you know. This is an excellent websiter to learn how to memorize your cords.

Synner

Synner is a website that gives you a pathway to starting your journey to the world of learning guitar.
